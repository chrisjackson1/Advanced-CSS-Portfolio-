Slice and Dice Project

Here's a project that I worked on with other collegues in the bootcamp..

This application is a Pizza ordering app where users can sign up or login to place an order while the employee receives those orders. This application was created to allow users to place an order online from wherever they are without having to call the restaurant or waiting in the store. It also helps the employer to gain more customers providing them with more orders to add to their in store orders.
